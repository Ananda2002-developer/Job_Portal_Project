About Job Portal

Job Portal is an online platform that connects job seekers with employers. It provides a space where individuals can create profiles, upload resumes, and search for job opportunities based on skills, location, or category. At the same time, companies can post job openings, manage applications, and find the right candidates efficiently. The portal simplifies the recruitment process, saves time, and makes it easier for both job providers and job seekers to meet their employment needs.
